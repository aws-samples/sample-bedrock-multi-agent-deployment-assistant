1. structural errors (missing keys, empty sections) — MUST fix
2. cfn-lint errors (property names, types, required fields) — MUST fix
3. cfn-guard errors (deployment best practices) — fix if possible
4. checkov warnings (security findings) — fix unless intentionally required by the product
